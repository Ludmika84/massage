This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Body Wisdom Sanctuary, operating a professional massage and aromatherapy practice in New York ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you visit our website, submit forms, call us, or receive our services. This Policy is designed to comply with applicable U.S. federal and state privacy laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CCPA/CPRA), the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA), the Colorado Privacy Act (CPA), the Connecticut Data Privacy Act (CTDPA), and other state laws where applicable.

This Policy does not apply to information collected offline at our studio, through third-party platforms we do not control, or on websites that link to or from our site. Those services are governed by their own policies.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information in the following categories:

Identifiers and contact information

  • Name, email address, phone number, and message content when you use our contact form;
  • Information you provide by phone, email, or in person when booking or inquiring about services.

Internet or other electronic network activity

  • Pages viewed, referring URL, browser type, device type, operating system, and approximate location derived from IP address;
  • Cookie identifiers and similar technologies (see our Cookie Policy);
  • Cookie consent preferences stored in your browser (local storage).

Communications and customer records

  • Records of correspondence, appointment requests, and service-related notes you choose to share;
  • Marketing preferences and opt-out requests.

Health-related information you voluntarily provide

If you disclose injuries, allergies, pregnancy, or other health information when booking or receiving massage services, we treat that information as sensitive and use it only to provide safe services, as described in Section 2. We do not use sensitive personal information to infer characteristics about you for unrelated purposes.

Sources of information

We collect information directly from you, automatically through your use of our website, from service providers that host or process forms on our behalf, and from embedded third-party content (such as Google Maps).

2. How We Use Personal Information

We use personal information for the following business and commercial purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries and processing booking or contact requests;
  • Providing massage, aromatherapy, and related wellness services;
  • Sending transactional messages (for example, appointment confirmations or responses to your messages);
  • Operating, maintaining, and improving our website and client experience;
  • Detecting security incidents, fraud, and abuse;
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Use;
  • With your consent or as permitted by law, sending promotional emails about our services.

5. Marketing Communications (CAN-SPAM Act)

If you receive marketing emails from us, each message will identify Body Wisdom Sanctuary as the sender and include a valid postal address and a clear way to unsubscribe. You may opt out of marketing emails at any time by:

  • Clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any promotional email; or
  • Emailing contact@bodywisdomsanctuary.com with the subject "Unsubscribe."

We process opt-out requests within 10 business days as required by the CAN-SPAM Act. Even if you opt out of marketing, we may still send transactional or service-related messages (for example, responses to your contact form submission).

6. Telephone Communications (TCPA)

If you provide a phone number, we may call or text you regarding appointments or inquiries you initiate. We will not send marketing text messages without your prior express consent where required by the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA). You may revoke consent to marketing texts at any time by replying STOP or contacting us directly.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly as required by the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Massage services for minors, if offered, require parental or guardian consent and supervision in accordance with applicable state law and studio policy.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including:

  • Service and appointment records: as required for business, tax, and legal purposes;
  • Marketing suppression lists: retained as long as needed to honor opt-out requests.

When information is no longer needed, we delete or de-identify it in accordance with our retention practices and applicable law.
