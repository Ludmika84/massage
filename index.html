Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about booking, pricing, and what to expect at Body Wisdom Sanctuary.

How do I book a session?

Use our online booking calendar, call +1 (347) 875-0819, or submit the contact form. We confirm your appointment by email or phone.

What are your prices?

Session rates depend on service type and duration. Current pricing is displayed on our online booking page before you confirm. We do not charge hidden fees.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please provide at least 24 hours notice to reschedule or cancel. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee, as stated at booking. This policy respects both clients and therapist time.

Is massage a medical treatment?

No. Our services are wellness and relaxation focused. They are not a substitute for medical diagnosis or treatment. Consult your doctor before booking if you have a health condition, injury, or pregnancy.
